About Me

California-based Pakistani paper artist with a love of paper, the written word and meandering outdoors. With a background in Economics and Finance and English, she is passionately doodles on any surface, weaves threads and is in love with paper and food. She loves the versatility and form that paper has to offer. In her own way, her expressions are cut and folded into her artwork.

When not surrounding by mounds of paper, she is wandering outside on her bike with crazy ideas churning in her head, reading thrillers or writing poetry.

My Vision

To merge the space we exist to another dimension with art that resonates.
